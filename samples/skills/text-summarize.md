# Text Summarization

Create a concise summary of provided text.

## Input
A long text passage (e.g., news article, documentation).

## Output
A 1–3 sentence summary capturing the key points.

## Example
Input:
```
The Amazon rainforest, often called the "lungs of the Earth," spans nine countries and encompasses
about 5.5 million square kilometers. It is home to approximately 10% of all species on Earth and
plays a crucial role in regulating global climate patterns...
```

Output:
```
The Amazon rainforest spans multiple countries across 5.5 million square kilometers and is home
to 10% of Earth's species while playing a critical role in global climate regulation.
```

## Notes
- Preserve factual accuracy
- Focus on main ideas, not minor details
- Target length: 1–3 sentences
